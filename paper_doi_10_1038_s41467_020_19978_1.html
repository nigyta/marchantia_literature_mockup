The liverwort oil body is formed by redirection of the secretory pathway.

Kanazawa, T., et al. · 2020 · Nature Communications   research

doi:10.1038/s41467-020-19978-1   PMID:33262353   PMC7708844

Summary

Shows that the liverwort oil body forms by redirection of the secretory pathway, with the ERF-type transcription factor MpERF13 controlling oil-body cell differentiation and a suite of SNARE/transport genes (MpSYP12A/B, MpVAMP71, etc.) marking the organelle.
