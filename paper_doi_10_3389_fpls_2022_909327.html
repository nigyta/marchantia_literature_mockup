Developmental Plasticity of the Amphibious Liverwort Riccia fluitans.

Althoff, F., et al. · 2022 · Fronteirs in Plant Science   research

doi:10.3389/fpls.2022.909327   PMID:35677239   PMC9168770

Summary

Characterises the developmental plasticity of the amphibious liverwort Riccia fluitans between submerged (water) and terrestrial (land) forms. M. polymorpha genes appear only as experimental tools (the proMpEF1α promoter and a GFP-MpTUB1 cell-division marker used in R. fluitans transgenics) or as comparative references cited from M. polymorpha literature (rhizoid, air-pore and cuticle regulators) — none are functionally studied here.
